Science-Informed Fueling: The P-Formance Rationale

Our approach is built on a deep understanding of carbohydrate metabolism and the challenges athletes face. Key scientific insights shape our fructose-free strategy:

‚ö†Ô∏èFructose & GI Distress Link

Research confirms that fructose ingested alone, or in non-optimized ratios, is linked to GI distress due to saturation of its GLUT5 transporter, leading to osmotic discomfort. This underpins our fructose-free commitment.

‚áÜCarbohydrate Absorption Pathways

Glucose/maltodextrin are absorbed via the robust SGLT1 transporter (~60-70 g/hr). Fructose relies on the lower-capacity GLUT5 transporter (~30 g/hr alone). P-Formance focuses on optimizing the SGLT1 pathway.

üîóUnderstanding MTC Strategies

Multiple Transportable Carbohydrate (MTC) formulas (glucose+fructose) aim to maximize CHO uptake. P-Formance offers a distinct alternative for those preferring to avoid fructose or whose needs align with efficient SGLT1 fueling.

üíßMaltodextrin's Role

Maltodextrin's lower sweetness and osmolality (vs. simple sugars) can improve palatability and GI tolerance, aligning with P-Formance's use of optimized maltodextrin/glucose blends for gut comfort.

By understanding these complexities, P-Formance consciously chooses a fructose-free path, aiming to provide the most reliable, gut-friendly, and effective fuel for a significant segment of the athletic population.
