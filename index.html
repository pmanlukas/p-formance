Science-Informed Fueling: The P-Formance Rationale

Our approach is built on a deep understanding of carbohydrate metabolism. Key scientific insights shape our fructose-free strategy:

How P-Formance Works: The SGLT1 Pathway

1. Ingestion: P-Formance fuel (Glucose/Maltodextrin based) is consumed.
↓
2. Intestinal Lumen: Glucose molecules arrive in the small intestine.
↓
3. SGLT1 Transporter Activation: Glucose binds to the SGLT1 transporter on intestinal cells.
↓
4. Efficient Absorption: Glucose transported into intestinal cell (capacity ~60-70g/hr).
↓
5. Enters Bloodstream: Glucose exits intestinal cell into bloodstream.
↓
6. Fuel for Muscles: Glucose readily available for working muscles.

P-Formance optimizes this pathway for efficient, gut-friendly energy, avoiding fructose/GLUT5 complexities.

⚠️Fructose & GI Distress Link

Research confirms fructose's link to GI distress via GLUT5 saturation. This underpins our fructose-free commitment.

🔗Understanding MTC Strategies

MTC (glucose+fructose) aims to max CHO uptake. P-Formance is a distinct alternative for those preferring fructose-free SGLT1 fueling.

💧Maltodextrin's Role for Gut Comfort

Maltodextrin's lower sweetness and osmolality aids palatability and GI tolerance, aligning with our optimized blends.

By understanding these complexities, P-Formance consciously chooses a fructose-free path.
